How to play

  • Print the cards on US Letter paper and cut along the dashed lines.
  • Place the cards face down. One player draws a card and acts it out silently.
  • Everyone else guesses. Set a 60-second timer for each turn.
  • For younger kids, let a grown-up read the card quietly to the actor.

144 cards included. Best for ages 4-10, family-safe play, classrooms, birthdays, and rainy-day activities.

License and Safety Notes

This printable pack is a curated children's activity set. Cards avoid adult, violent, scary, political, and brand-specific prompts.

The card art uses original watercolor-style illustrations composed into a consistent printable vintage card template.

Print tip: use “Actual size” or “100% scale” in your printer dialog so the cut lines stay aligned.
